About Souhoola

Souhoola is Egypt's leading BNPL (Buy Now, Pay Later) platform, making purchases accessible and affordable for everyone.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize consumer finance in Egypt, Souhoola provides flexible payment solutions that empower millions of Egyptians to purchase what they need, when they need it. Our innovative platform connects customers with thousands of merchants across the country, offering seamless installment plans with transparent terms.

We believe that financial flexibility should be accessible to all. That's why we've built a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of our customers' needs, creating a shopping experience that's both convenient and trustworthy.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the most leading consumer finance platform empowering individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals and support financial inclusion in the Egyptian Market.

Our Mission

We provide accessible, reliable, and innovative digital financial services through a customer-centric approach and a fintech platform. We aim to build long lasting relationships with our customers and offer a strong customer experience journey based on trust and transparency. We aim to simplify financial processes and adopt an advanced innovation and technology concepts . By leveraging cutting edge technology , data analytics and customer excellence , we strive to be a trusted partner in supporting individuals and business to thrive financially. We strive to make financial management easier , attain financial inclusion and stability.
